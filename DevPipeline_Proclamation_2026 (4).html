Proclamation of Achievement & Purpose
Whereas, DevPipeline is not a school, not a bootcamp, and not a staffing agency — it is America's only competency-based, Demand-Side technology apprenticeship: simultaneously an Educator, Employer, and Workforce Intermediary, headquartered in Orem, Utah, with offices in Vernal, Utah and Burbank, California, and expanding across Sanpete, Duchesne, Uintah, Utah, Salt Lake, Carbon, and Emery Counties;

Whereas, DevPipeline's Demand-Side model ends the "train and pray" cycle — apprentices are hired at Week 15, paid on real client projects, and face zero days of job searching, because DevPipeline and its employer partners are not waiting to see if graduates find work — they are the work;
